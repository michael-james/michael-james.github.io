CommunityCrit

CommunityCrit, a platform created by a team from UCSD’s Design Lab, takes a variety of urban planning documents and presents them to the public in a way that allows for quick yet meaningful contributions.

The Park to Park Project

The master plan for the 14th Street Promenade, which will connect City College in the north to Barrio Logan in the south, was approved by Civic San Diego in 2016. With the goal of creating a more sustainable, walkable downtown, the 14th Street Promenade will feature widened sidewalks, outdoor seating, and art in order to promote social gathering and community expression.

An addition to this project—extending the 14th Street Promenade from City College to Balboa Park in the north and from the intersection of 14th Street and National Avenue to Chicano Park in the south—is currently being considered.

This platform offers you the opportunity to contribute your feedback about how the Park-to-Park extension relates to mobility, neighborhood identity, the economy, and more for project organizers and the public to view.
